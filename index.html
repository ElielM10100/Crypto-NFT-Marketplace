FAQ

What is an NFT?

An NFT (non-fungible token) is a unique digital asset that represents ownership of a specific item or piece of content on the blockchain.

How do I buy an NFT?

You can purchase NFTs through various marketplaces using cryptocurrencies like Ethereum.

Can I sell my NFTs?

Yes, you can sell your NFTs on different platforms and marketplaces where they are supported.
